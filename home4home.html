when life doesn’t go as planned.

When life doesn’t go as planned, finding somewhere safe, comfortable and welcoming shouldn’t add to the stress.

Our Home from Home service helps individuals and families who need temporary accommodation at short notice. Whether you’re relocating, facing an unexpected housing situation, or simply between homes, we help you find a place that feels secure, supportive, and genuinely like home.

We provide short-to-medium-term accommodation solutions designed to give you stability while you plan your next steps. Our homes are fully prepared so you can focus on what matters most — you, your family, your wellbeing, and moving forward.

We work with people who need accommodation for many different reasons.

You may be:

  • Between homes
  • Relocating for work or study
  • Facing an unexpected housing situation
  • Needing a short-term stop gap for you and your family
