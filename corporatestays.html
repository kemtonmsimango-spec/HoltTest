Our Guarantee:

At Holt Accommodation Solutions, we make sure every property meets a consistent minimum standard — so your teams can settle in properly and focus on the job from day one.

We guarantee every stay includes:

  • Clean, well-maintained homes suitable for comfortable daily living
  • One bedroom per person for privacy and rest
  • At least one bathroom for every two guests
  • A fully equipped kitchen for everyday cooking
  • On-site laundry facilities
  • Reliable WiFi and a TV for downtime and communication
  • Parking for at least one vehicle where required

Across all our properties in the UK, EU, and beyond, we deliver consistent, dependable accommodation for working teams, contractors, and crews — wherever the job takes you.
